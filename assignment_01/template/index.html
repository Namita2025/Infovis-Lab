Conclusion

Vegan meals at Mensa Vaihingen emit significantly less CO₂ and cost less on average than vegetarian or non-vegetarian options. However, they also contain the least protein per portion. Choosing sustainably does mean spending less — but comes with a nutritional trade-off worth considering.
